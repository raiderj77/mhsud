# mindchecktools.com — Brand Voice

## Site Overview
mindchecktools.com is a free mental health and substance use screening tool suite.
Tools: PHQ-9 (depression), GAD-7 (anxiety), AUDIT (alcohol use), Mental Load Calculator, and more.
Monetization: Google AdSense. Cookiebot CMP installed (CBID: a9a99ccb-4863-4e33-a895-a6d5642f408d).
YMYL site — all content requires extra care and clinical accuracy.

## Target Audience
- Adults seeking self-awareness about their mental health
- People supporting a loved one with mental health challenges
- Clinicians looking for quick screening tool references
- Students in health and counseling fields

## Tone
- Warm, non-judgmental, and supportive
- Clinical accuracy without clinical coldness
- Empowering — never alarmist, never dismissive
- Approachable to general public (8th grade reading level)

## Messaging Framework
- Lead with empathy: acknowledge that seeking information is a positive step
- Provide clear, factual information
- Always normalize help-seeking behavior
- Never diagnose or prescribe — always refer to professionals

## Voice Pillars
1. **Compassionate** — the reader may be in distress
2. **Accurate** — no misinformation on mental health topics
3. **Empowering** — information helps people make informed decisions
4. **Appropriately cautious** — YMYL compliance is non-negotiable

## YMYL Rules (Non-Negotiable)
- Every article must include the crisis resources block (988, Crisis Text Line, SAMHSA)
- Every article must include: "This article is for informational purposes only..."
- Every article must include attribution: "Reviewed by a Certified Drug and Alcohol Counselor (CADC-II)"
- Never make diagnostic claims
- Never recommend specific medications or treatments
- Always recommend professional consultation

## Attribution
- Blog posts: "Reviewed by a Certified Drug and Alcohol Counselor (CADC-II)"
- Tool pages only: "Jason Ramirez, CADC-II" (for E-E-A-T)
- No author name in blog post bylines
