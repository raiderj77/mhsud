# mindchecktools.com — SEO Guidelines

## Primary Content Focus
Mental health screening, depression and anxiety self-assessment, substance use awareness, stress management, emotional wellbeing.

## YMYL Requirements
This is a YMYL (Your Money or Your Life) site. Google holds health content to the highest E-E-A-T standards.
- Every article needs an expert review attribution
- Cite published clinical sources (DSM-5, NIMH, SAMHSA, peer-reviewed journals)
- No speculative health claims
- Recommend professional help in every article

## Keyword Strategy
- Target: "what is [condition]", "[screening tool] score meaning", "signs of [condition]", "how to help someone with [condition]"
- Avoid: competing directly with Mayo Clinic, WebMD on broad symptom queries
- Focus on: screening tool-adjacent content where we have tool authority

## Internal Linking
Link to the relevant screening tool page in every article where appropriate.
Example: article about depression → link to PHQ-9 tool.

## Meta Title Format
`[Topic] | Mental Health Information | MindCheckTools`

## Schema
Use MedicalWebPage schema. Use FAQPage schema on FAQ sections. Use Article schema with author entity.

## GEO (AI Citation) Requirements
- 40–60 word direct answer blocks under every H2
- Include clinical definitions (LLMs frequently cite clinical content)
- Structured FAQ sections with real user questions
