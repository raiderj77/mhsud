# mindchecktools.com — Style Guide

## Formatting Rules
- H1: One per page with primary keyword
- H2: 4–7 per article
- Paragraphs: 2–4 sentences, no walls of text
- Use bullet lists for symptoms, signs, strategies
- Callout boxes for crisis resources (use blockquote > format in Markdown)

## Terminology Preferences
- "mental health challenges" not "mental illness" (less stigmatizing)
- "substance use disorder" not "addiction" (clinical preference)
- "screening tool" not "test" or "quiz"
- "professional support" not "therapy" (broader; therapy has specific meaning)
